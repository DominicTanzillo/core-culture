Our why

We started this because chips were lying to us.

Maya, our co-founder, was reading the back of a "healthy" chip bag at 1am during her senior year and counted thirty-four ingredients. None of them were potato. Most of them ended in -ate.

We figured if we wouldn't put it in our bodies, we wouldn't put it in a bag with our name on it. So we made our own. Whole grains. Real veg. Olive oil. Spice. That's it.

We're a six-person team in Cincinnati. We answer our own DMs. We compost the test batches. We're trying to build the kind of snack company we wished existed when we were 19.

— Maya & Jules Co-founders
