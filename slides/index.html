01 / The Problem

Healthy snacks are still lying.

Gen Z is the most health-aware generation ever — and the most frustrated. The "better-for-you" aisle is full of seed oils, hidden sugars, and ingredient lists longer than a pharmacy receipt.

  • 78% of Gen Z read labels before buying. (Mintel, 2025)
  • 62% distrust "natural" claims from legacy brands.
  • $48B salty-snack market, growing 6% YoY — most innovation is theater.

03 / The Solution

KERNL: chips your grandparents would recognize.

Whole-grain corn + ancient grains, fried in olive and avocado oil, seasoned with real food. Seven ingredients or fewer. Always.

  • Radically short ingredient list
  • No seed oils, no added sugar, no isolates
  • Real veg as flavor (beet, avocado, lime — not powder)
  • Home-compostable bag (TÜV-certified)
  • Made in Cincinnati. Founder-led. Stays small on purpose.
